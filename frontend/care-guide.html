Paithani Care Guide

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Everyday Handling

Handle Paithani sarees with clean, dry hands. Avoid heavy perfume or direct spray on the fabric.

  • Wear the saree after makeup and perfume application.
  • Avoid sharp jewellery that can snag the weave or zari.

Cleaning

We recommend dry cleaning only for Paithani sarees and accessories to protect the silk and zari.

  • Do not machine wash or soak.
  • Use a trusted dry cleaner experienced with silk.

Stain Care

For light stains, gently blot with a clean, dry cloth. Do not rub.

  • Act quickly to prevent setting.
  • Consult a dry cleaner for anything beyond light surface marks.

Storage

Store your Paithani in a breathable cotton or muslin cloth, away from direct sunlight.

  • Avoid plastic covers for long-term storage.
  • Refold the saree every few months to prevent crease stress.

Ironing and Steaming

Use low heat with a protective cotton layer. Avoid direct heat on zari.

  • Iron on the reverse side when possible.
  • Steam lightly from a distance if needed.

Travel Tips

Roll the saree in soft tissue or muslin to reduce creasing during travel.

  • Keep it in a cool, dry bag.
  • Avoid heavy pressure on the folds.
